About

I am an Integrated Science teacher at Philippine Science High School. I am developing an Integrated Science course that is free from traditional disciplinary boundaries-integrating concepts and principles from Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Earth Science within unified and coherent learning sessions. My research interests mainly focus on improving Science teaching, including techniques such as Flipped Classroom, Differentiated Instruction, and Community Based Learning in Secondary Science. As a middle administrator, I monitor students with a variety of abilities, talents, and challenges, including mental health concerns. My advocacy for students' mental health, academic motivation, and well-being inspired me to research on this field as I pursue my Doctor of Education in QUT.
